Movement
--------
- Left-click to walk toward the cursor. With "Click-to-Toggle Walk" on, one click sets a target.
- You can also move with WASD or the arrow keys. Hold Shift to run.

Communication
-------------
- Press Enter to start typing and Enter again to send.
- Press Esc to cancel. Use Up or Down to browse previous messages.

UI Navigation
-------------
- Use the Windows button to open or close windows.
- Scroll the mouse wheel to move through lists and text windows.

Extras
------
- Put `background.png` or `splash.png` in the data folder to change the look.
- To try music, add a soundfont and use `/play <instrument> <notes>`.
